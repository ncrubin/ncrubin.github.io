Variational 2-RDM on Models of Antiferromagnets

Date Wed 03 February 2016

In 2014 David Mazziotti and I published a paper on the performance of approximate N-representability at capturing the electronic structure of the Hubbard model on linear and ladder lattices for a range of interaction parameters.

The ground state 2-RDM is calculated with variational 2-RDM with two sets of approximate N-representability. We validate the approximate N-representability by comparing the electronic structure probed with charge-order (\(\langle \hat{n}_{i\alpha}\hat{n}_{i+R,\beta}\rangle\)), effective transport, and orbital occupation numbers compared against the exact solution (FCI).

This was my first successful project with variational 2-RDM theory and the first steps towards simulating the electronic structure of strongly correlated materials.
